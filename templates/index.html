ABOUT US

Global Green Concepts Pvt Ltd. (GGC) is uniquely positioned as an Environment Solutions Provider with over fifteen years experience in one major business domain - Water & Environment.


LEADERS IN WATER MANAGEMENT

Global Green Concepts Pvt Ltd. (GGC) is uniquely positioned as an Environment Solutions Provider with over fifteen years experience in one major business domain - Water & Environment.

We are internationally recognized with references in the entire gamut of environmental solutions including, but not limited to: Sewage & Effluent Treatment Technologies, Water Conservation Methods,etc.

BEST-IN-CLASS SOLUTIONS

True to its title, GGC focuses entirely on offering integrated solutions for all environment engineering related problems and issues. The end result is that your business and your environment thrive hand in hand helping to sustain each other.
Our core strength’s include consulting in the water space, along with turnkey solutions for creating water-related infrastructure.

INTERNATIONALLY RECOGNIZED

We are the licensee for the world renowned Anox Kaldnes MBBR™ process invented and patented by Anox Kaldnes, a Swedish/Norwegian conglomerate with several intellectual properties in the field of waste water treatment technology.

We provide wastewater treatment solutions for all types of applications, from compact domestic systems to large-scale turnkey solutions, including feasibility studies
